About Me

I am a 40 year old male native to Utah. I was born in Provo. I moved to Elgin, Illinois (with my parents...obviously) before entering kindergarten. At the end of the school year we took a big trip to Hawaii! We lived on Oahu in Kalama Valley until I was almost finished with the 5th grade. Then paradise came to a halt and we were back in my birth state, enjoying snow once again.

I married my high school sweetheart, Jenni, in 2001. We have had three children since then. The oldest, Kaleb, is 15. Our 2nd, Kaiya, is 13. And our youngest, Katelin, is 10 years old. These kids keep us busy and make us happy.
